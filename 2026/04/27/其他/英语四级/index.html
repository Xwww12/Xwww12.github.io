技巧

做题习惯：

  • 先看题，默读，将每个选项都有的单词划掉（不用关注）
  • 边听边划线，听到什么划什么
  • 选被读到最多的选项
  • 整句被读到的 > 部分被读到的
  • 同义替换
  • 题项中有代词时可听问题来确定所指物，再选选项

短片新闻：

  • ✨导语出题：第一句话可能概括了主要内容，极易出题
  • 一般都是顺序出题，两题答案之间相隔不远

长对话：

  • 锁定选项特殊词
  • 锁定选项主语，弄清代词指的是谁
  • 注意but、however、because等，容易引出答案
  • 选完直接下一题，不要迟疑
  • 答案可能在有重复出现的内容的选项里

短文：

  • 篇幅较长
  • 分值高，一题等于前面的两题
  • 仔细听开篇和结尾
  • At first，firstly，beginning等词后面可能出考题

落款

表达方式

增长/下降

  • 加拿大的工业产值增加了6%

    Canada’s industrial output increased by 6%.

  • 从1995到2000年加拿大工业产值增加了6%

    There was an increase of 6% in Canada’s industrial output from 1995 to 2000.

  • 销售额从5千美元极速增长到超过10万美元

    Sales increased dramatically from five thousand dollars to over 10 thousand dollars.

  • 与1980年相比，2010年的年轻人口的占比几乎下降了17%

    The proportion of young population in 2010 almost declined by 17 percent, compared with the year 1980.

  • 我们可以发现房价在1990年到1995年下降 。

    We can see that house prices fell between 1990 and 1995.

范文

2015.6 卷2 图画

As is vividly demonstrated in the cartoon, a young girl is chatting with her mother. The daughter tells her mother, “Good news mom! I was accepted to the college of your choice.” Obviously, the girl believes that her mother’s choice is the best choice.

It is such a common thing nowadays that most parents tend to make decisions for their children, including choosing colleges and majors, hunting for a job or getting married. Several reasons account for this phenomenon. First of all, parents always think that they are able to make a wiser choice than teenagers. In addition, most parents in China only have one child, so they attach too much attention to their only child in the family.

Accordingly, it is necessary for us to derive positive implication from the symbolic picture. Children should be allowed to determine their own future. In the meantime, parents should offer some helpful suggestions for the youngster.

翻译：

正如这幅漫画生动展现的那样，一个小女孩正和母亲聊天。女儿告诉母亲：“妈妈，好消息！我被你选的大学录取了。” 显然，这个女孩认为母亲的选择就是最好的选择。

如今，大多数父母都倾向于为子女做决定，这已是十分普遍的现象，这些决定包括选大学和专业、找工作甚至结婚。造成这一现象的原因有很多。首先，父母总觉得自己能比青少年做出更明智的选择。此外，中国的大多数父母只有一个孩子，因此他们会对家中唯一的孩子投入过多的关注。

因此，我们有必要从这幅具有象征意义的图画中汲取积极的启示：应当让孩子自主决定自己的未来；同时，父母也应当为年轻人提供一些有益的建议。

2015.6 卷1 图画

From the picture above we can see that a person is using his computer all day, whether he is working, studying or playing. Even when he is sleeping, the computer is also in his dream. Obviously, the person thinks that he can not live without the computer even for a minute.

It is such a common thing nowadays that people tend to consider the computer as a necessity. From my perspective, computers play an important but not indispensable role in our modern life. On the one hand, it is true that the computer is one of the most helpful tools not only to meet the work’s needs, but also to entertain oneself. But on the other hand, excessive addiction to the computer may have a negative impact on our health, especially on our eyes.

Hence, it is necessary for us to derive positive implication from the symbolic picture. Computers are invented to promote our life not to harm our health. Therefore, it is necessary for us to use computers in a reasonable way.

翻译：

从上图中我们可以看到，一个人整天都在使用电脑，无论是工作、学习还是娱乐。甚至在睡觉时，电脑也出现在他的梦里。显然，这个人觉得自己一分钟都离不开电脑。
