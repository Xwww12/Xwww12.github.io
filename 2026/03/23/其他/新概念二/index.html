单词

语法

on/in/at + 时间

结构：

  • on + 具体某一天
  • in + 年/季/月
  • at + 时间点

例：

  • See you on Friday
  • The concert is on 22 November
  • I’m going on holiday in October
  • Emma left school in 1993
  • I start work at 8 o’clock
  • At midnight, they were still studying

直接引语变间接引语

时态往之前变化

直接引语 间接引语 直接引语 间接引语
am/is/are was/were can could
am/is/are +doing was/were +doing may might
do/does did will would
have/has +done had+done shall should
did had+done must had to

例：

  • I never work on Sundays. What did he say?

    He said he never worked on Sunday.

  • I broke that plate. What did he tell you?

    He told me he had broken that plate.

  • I didn’t see Tom. What did he say?

    He said he had not seen Tom.

  • I can go there tomorrow. What did he say?

    He told me he could go there tomorrow.

  • I haven’t heard from him.

    He told me he hadn’t heard from him.

一定 / 一定不（过去）

结构：

must have done / been：一定是…（过去，主观）

can’t have done / been：一定不是…（过去，客观）

例：

  • They mush have done out.
  • I must have been sleeping.
  • She can’t have seen me.
  • He can’t have been watching TV.

可能（过去）

结构：

may / might (not) have done（过去）

may / might (not) have been（过去）

may / might (not) have been doing（过去）

例：

  • She may have been asleep
  • You might have left in the shop
  • He may not have been feeling well

动名词

动词 + ing 变名词

注意：

  • 作为主语时为单数

例：

  • Fishing makes me happy
  • Sleeping is good for you
  • Seeing is beliving

情态动词的被动

结构：

  • 一般现在时：am/is/are done
  • 一般过去时：was/were done
  • 现在进行时：am/is/are being done
  • 现在完成时：have/has been done
  • 过去完成时：had been done
  • 一定（过去）：must have been done

例：

  • My watch must have been stolen
  • This room must have been cleaned

并列连词

连接两个句子/单词

  1. and, bot, or, so

  2. both…and：两个都

    Both Leo and his GF like to watch movies

    Both my wife and I are going to Pairs

    He can both sing and dance

  3. either…or：要么…要么…

    Either the teacher or the students are wrong

    When the girl is happy, she either sings or dances

  4. neither…nor：两个都没有

    Neither dad nor mom is at home today.

    I want neither this one nor that one.

  5. not only…but also/(… as well)：不仅…而且

    Not only the doctor but also the patients does not know about it.

    You must not only wash the dishes but sweep the floor as well.

done做定语

动词的过去分词作为定语使用，

通常表示 被动一般/被动完成

例：

  • on a ploughed filed：在梨过的地上
  • deserted car park：废弃的停车场
  • a broken car：打碎的杯子

doing做定语

动词的现在分词作为定语使用，

主动进行/主动一般

例：

  • The sinking ship
  • The rising sun
  • The sleeping child

use to do

过去常常，现在不是了

例：

  • He used to smoke
  • I used to be a student
  • She used to have long hair when she was a girl
  • She used to be a burse, but she isn’t a nurse any more

would

过去重复发生的动作，现在怎样不一定

例：

  • I would smoke
  • When Leo was angry, he would always walk out of the room

as … as

和…一样，像…一样

结构：

  • 肯定句：as … as
  • 否定句：not as(或者so) … as

例：

  • The blue car is as clean as the red car
  • He is not as/so honest as his father

so such

强调程度

用法：

  • so + 形容词
  • such + (形容词) + 名词

例：

  • The story was so stupid
  • It was a such stupid story

so/such .. that

如此…以至于

例：

  • The ball struck him so hard that he nearly fall into the water
  • It was such a good book that I could’n put it down.

no sooner … than

一… 就…

结构：

  • 主 + had no sonner done than + 句子(did)

例：

  • He had no sooner returned than he bought a house and setted down in the country

hardly … when

还没来得及 … 就

结构：

  • 主 + had hardly done when + 句子(did)

例：

  • He had hardly had time to settle down when he sold the house and left the country

虚拟语气

从句的动作和现在的事实相反或发生概率很低

结构：

  • If + did/were, 主 + would/should/could/might do

结构：

  • If I were you, I wouldn’t do that.
  • If I knew them better, I might invite them to dinner

As

根据上下文有because、when的意思

介词后面加动词

统一动词要加ing

常见介词：before /after/without /by/in /on/at

例：

  • He hesitated for a moment before opening the door

    等同于…before he opened the door.

the way

以这种方式

例：

  • I don’t need to do it the way you did：我不需要像你那样做
  • I like the way you talk.

时态

现在进行时

还有近期计划、安排的意思

例：

  • I’m meeting my friends tonight

to do sth.

句子末尾加 to do sth.表示目的

现在完成时

结构：have + done

用法：

  1. 过去的事情对现在的影响 ≈ did
  2. 从过去持续到现在

例：

  • He has cleanes his shoes（鞋现在是干净的）
  • I have just had some food（现在不饿）
  • I have lived in Beijing for 10 years
  • I haven’t eaten anything since breakfast

过去/现在/将来完成时对比：

对比维度 现在完成时 过去完成时 将来完成时
核心结构 主语 + have/has + 过去分词 主语 + had + 过去分词 主语 + will have + 过去分词
时间立足点 站在 “现在” 站在 “过去某个时间点” 站在 “将来某个时间点”
核心含义 到现在为止已完成；动作对现在有影响 到过去某件事之前已完成（过去的过去） 到将来某一时间点前已完成
常用时间标志 already、yet、ever、never、so far、for + 时间段、since + 时间点 by + 过去时间、before + 过去动作、when/after + 过去从句 by + 将来时间（如 by tomorrow/2025）、by the time + 将来从句

将来完成时

到将来某个时间点就会已经做完

结构：

  • 主语 + will have + 过去分词

例：

  • By tomorrow, I will have finished my homework.
  • By next year, I will have graduated.

现在完成进行时

  1. 过去进行的动作（现在结束了），对现在有影响
  2. 过去进行的动作持续到现在（同have done）

结构：

  • 主 + have/has been doing sth.

例：

  • Pual is very tired. He has been working very hard.

  • Your eyes look red. What happened?

    I have been watching TV.

  • You are out of breath

    Have you been running？

  • It’s has been raining for two hours（持续）
